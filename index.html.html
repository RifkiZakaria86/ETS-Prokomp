Research

Research is the process of systematically collecting, analyzing and translating information or data on a problem, be it a company in all fields or a mid-level entrepreneur, namely business, which aims to increase our understanding of a particular phenomenon that attracts our attention to the problem at hand.

Data Analyst

Data analysis is a process for classifying, seeing relationships, making comparisons, similarities and differences on data that is ready to be studied, and creating visualization data models on company or business problems with the intention of finding useful information so that it can provide guidance for making decisions about solutions to problems faced by companies or businesses

Inovation

Innovation in this case is the final stage after conducting research and analysis of data obtained from problems of an existing company or business, namely a decision to be made or a new thing developed or created to maximize a company's profit so that the problems faced are resolved and can be minimized for the progress of existing companies or businesses
